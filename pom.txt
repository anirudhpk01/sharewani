<!-- explanation box -->
<rect x="460" y="440" width="550" height="148" rx="8" fill="none" stroke="#C0C0BB" stroke-width="0.5"/>
<text font-size="11" font-weight="500" x="476" y="460" fill="#1a1a18" font-family="system-ui,sans-serif">How to read this diagram</text>
<text font-size="11" x="476" y="478" fill="#5F5E5A" font-family="system-ui,sans-serif">A queue is a waiting line of items.</text>
<text font-size="11" x="476" y="494" fill="#5F5E5A" font-family="system-ui,sans-serif">A worker is a background process that pulls from that line.</text>
<text font-size="11" x="476" y="510" fill="#5F5E5A" font-family="system-ui,sans-serif">A 100ms batch window means: first item arrives → worker waits</text>
<text font-size="11" x="476" y="524" fill="#5F5E5A" font-family="system-ui,sans-serif">up to 100ms to collect nearby items → processes them together.</text>
